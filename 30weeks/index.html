Intro

The CliffsNotes: Studied psychology, philosophy, and music at Emory University. Experimented with IT, creative brand advertising, and education inside of big corps, boutiques, and startups (including, oddly enough, a school that was a startup). Now, an entrepreneurial designer and engineer that enjoys producing top-quality products with harmonious teams.

I care about long-term product and business strategies that result in overall net-good for stakeholders, producers, and customers.

Day-to-day I enjoy thinking big and small: While always looking to connect ideas from disparate professional and academic domains, I'm also interested in mastering the craft of code and design.

In my view, ideas and execution are equally important. Lacking in either results in a failed product, ameaningless product, or simply nothing at all.
